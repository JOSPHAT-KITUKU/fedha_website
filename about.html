Fedha Brothers CBO

Mission

Fedha Brothers is a Community-Based Organization (CBO) with a core mission of fostering unity, providing educational programs, and ensuring equitable access to essential resources for individuals and families within its community.

Vision

The central purpose of Fedha Brothers is to create a thriving community grounded in the values of unity, compassion, and social responsibility. By offering educational programs and facilitating access to essential resources, the organization aims to uplift individuals and families, fostering a sense of belonging and well-being across its members.

Objectives

Fedha Brothers has defined its objectives to guide its work and impact within the community:

1. Education Programs:

To provide educational programs that promote a deeper understanding of Islam, enriching the lives of individuals within the community.

2. Unity and Social Responsibility:

To create engaging outreach programs that promote unity, personal growth, and a shared sense of social responsibility.

3. Support for the Less Fortunate:

To assist the less fortunate members of society by offering essential resources such as food, shelter, healthcare, education, entrepreneurship training, and employment opportunities.

Meet Our Team

Team

Fedha Brothers is governed by a Board of Directors, which consists of 12 members. The Board is responsible for the overall management and strategic decision-making of the organization. The structure is designed to ensure efficiency in leadership and accountability across all operations.
