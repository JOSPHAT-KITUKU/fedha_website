Fedha Brothers CBO Membership Procedure

Eligibility

Fedhp Brothers CBO Membership is open to individuals who align with the vision and mission of Fedha Brothers, regardless of race, gender, nationality, or social background.

Admission

Individuals can become members of Fedha Brothers CBO through participation in activities and formal admission. Members are free to leave at any time if they are not holding an official position.

Rights and Responsibilities

Fedha Brothers CBO Members have the right to attend events, receive updates, and participate in the organization’s activities. In return, they are expected to uphold the organization’s principles and values, contribute actively to its mission, and promote unity within the community.
