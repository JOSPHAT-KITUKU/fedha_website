Fedha Brothers Community-Based Organization (CBO)

Who Are we

An organization that stands as a beacon of support and empowerment, impacting lives and contributing to the creation of a harmonious and prosperous society.

Mission

An Organization fostering unity, providing educational programs, and ensuring equitable access to essential resources for individuals and families within its community

Our Core Projects

Trainings

We organize various training programs including Madrasa sessions, First Aid training for volunteers, and personal development workshops to uplift knowledge and practical skills in our community.

Empowerment Programs

Through mentorship, entrepreneurship support, and vocational skills training, we empower youth and women to become self-reliant and confident contributors to society.

Financial Support

We offer urgent financial aid including debt clearance and assistance with hospital bills for individuals and families in dire need.

Food Distribution

Our initiatives include regular food distribution drives—especially during Ramadhan—to support vulnerable families and ensure no one in our community goes hungry.

Street Dawah

We engage in public awareness campaigns that promote understanding, unity, and the values of Islam, reaching out to people from all backgrounds with compassion and respect.

Livelihood Support Initiatives

We provide start-up support, business mentorship, and tools to help community members start or expand their income-generating activities.
